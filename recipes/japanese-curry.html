Japanese Curry recipe

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Protein of your choice in chunks
  • curry blocks
  • Couple cups of water/stock

Steps

  1. brown your vegetables in a pan
  2. cover ingredients with water and let simmer until ingredients are tender
  3. turn the heat off and stir in curry blocks
  4. serve over a bowl of white rice and enjoy!
