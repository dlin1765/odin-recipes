Cabbage stirfry recipe

Ingredients

  • cabbage hand torn
  • chinese dried red chilis
  • protein of your choice
  • soy sauce
  • oyester sauce
  • msg
  • garlic

Steps

  1. first, get the wok piping hot
  2. and as always... longyao...
  3. add oil to the wok until it smokes
  4. add protein and cook until nearly cooked
  5. remove the protein and set aside
  6. add cabbage and cook until almost tender
  7. add soy sauce, oyster, msg, protein, and garlic on the edges of the wok
  8. stir to combine
  9. aaaand... out.
